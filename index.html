🃏 Singapore Mahjong — Official Rules

Tiles

  • 144 tiles total
  • 3 Suits × 9 values × 4 copies = 108 (Characters/Man, Circles/Pin, Bamboo/Sou)
  • 4 Wind tiles × 4 copies = 16 (East, South, West, North)
  • 3 Dragon tiles × 4 copies = 12 (Red 中, Green 發, White 白)
  • 4 Flower bonus tiles × 1 = 4 (Flower 1–4, one per seat)
  • 4 Season bonus tiles × 1 = 4 (Season 1–4, one per seat)
  • 4 Animal bonus tiles × 1 = 4 (Cat, Mouse, Rooster, Worm)

Hand Structure

Standard hand = 4 melds + 1 pair (14 tiles total)
A meld is a Sequence (3 consecutive same-suit) or a Triplet (3 identical tiles).
A Kang (4 identical tiles) counts as a meld but requires drawing 1 replacement tile, increasing hand size to 15 (or more for multiple Kangs).
Bonus tiles (Flowers, Seasons, Animals) are drawn separately and are never part of the 14-tile count.

Dealer & Wind Rotation

  • East (Player 1) is the dealer in Round 1. East Wind is the prevailing wind.
  • If East (dealer) wins → winds do NOT rotate. Same dealer plays again.
  • If any other player wins → all seat winds rotate anti-clockwise one step.
  • After every player has held East once (4 wind changes), the prevailing wind changes: East → South → West → North.
  • A full game = all 4 players complete their turn as dealer = one complete "round cycle".

Traditional Singapore Scoring (Payment)

Points formula: 2^(fan). Minimum 0 fan = 1 point (Chicken Hand).

Discard Win:
— Discarder pays winner: 2× base points
— Each of the other 2 non-winning, non-discarding players pays winner: 1× base points
— Total received by winner: 4× base points

Self Draw (Zimo) — more profitable:
— Each of the 3 losing players pays winner: 2× base points
— Total received by winner: 6× base points

Flowers, Seasons & Animals

  • There are exactly 4 Flower tiles (Flower 1–4), 4 Season tiles (Season 1–4), and 4 Animal tiles (Cat, Mouse, Rooster, Worm) — one of each.
  • Bonus tiles are drawn when a player draws one — they place it aside and draw a replacement from the wall.
  • Flower 1 & Season 1 belong to the East seat player (+1 fan each if held by matching seat).
  • All 4 Flowers collected = +3 fan; All 4 Seasons collected = +3 fan (separate bonuses).
  • Animals: Cat pairs with Mouse (+1 fan per pair); Rooster pairs with Worm (+1 fan per pair); All 4 Animals = +3 fan.
  • Non-own Flower/Season (wrong seat) = +1 fan each.

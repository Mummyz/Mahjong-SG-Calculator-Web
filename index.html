Singapore Mahjong — Official Rules

Based on official Singaporean Mahjong scoring rules (148 tiles). Source: singaporemahjong.com

Tile Set (148 tiles total)

  • Suited tiles: 3 suits × 9 values × 4 copies = 108 (Characters 萬, Circles 筒, Bamboo 索)
  • Wind tiles: East 東, South 南, West 西, North 北 — 4 copies each = 16
  • Dragon tiles: Red 中 (Chun), Green 發 (Fa), White 白 (Pai) — 4 copies each = 12
  • Flower tiles: 4 tiles — Plum 梅, Orchid 蘭, Chrysanthemum 菊, Bamboo 竹 (one each)
  • Season tiles: 4 tiles — Spring 春, Summer 夏, Autumn 秋, Winter 冬 (one each)
  • Animal tiles: 4 tiles — Cat 🐱, Rat/Mouse 🐭, Rooster 🐦, Centipede/Worm 🪱 (one each)

Dealer (East) and opposite player (West) each have 19 stacks. South and North have 18 stacks. Dead wall = 15 tiles.

Hand Structure

Standard winning hand = 4 sets + 1 pair (14 tiles)

  • Chow (吃) — Sequence of 3 consecutive tiles, same suit only
  • Pong (碰) — Triplet of 3 identical tiles
  • Kang (杠) — 4 identical tiles; requires drawing 1 replacement tile (hand becomes 15, 16... tiles)
  • Bonus tiles (Flowers, Seasons, Animals) are drawn separately — never counted in the 14-tile hand
  • Minimum requirement: 1 point to win (起胡 qǐ-hú)

Scoring Formula

Base value doubles with each point: 1pt = 1×, 2pt = 2×, 3pt = 4×, 4pt = 8×, 5pt(Limit) = 16×

Players agree a base monetary value for 1 point before the game (e.g. S$1).

Flower, Season & Animal Bonus Rules

  • Own Flower/Season (正花): East holds Flower 1 or Season 1, South holds Flower 2 or Season 2, etc. = +1pt each.
  • Other Flower/Season (偏花): Non-matching seat flower tiles score 0 points toward the hand in standard rules.
  • Complete Flower Set (一台花): All 4 Flowers of same colour = +2pts (1 for own + 1 for complete). Instant payout to all players when collected.
  • All Flowers & Seasons (八仙过海): Collect all 8 bonus tiles by Self-Draw = automatic win at limit. Do NOT reveal hidden hand.
  • Animal Instant Payments (咬到 yǎo-dào): Cat+Mouse pair OR Rooster+Worm pair = immediate one-time payment from all players. All 4 animals = double payment (cumulative). This is separate from the end-game score.
  • Animal points: Each animal = +1pt. All 4 animals = +5pts total (4+1 bonus).

Dealer & Wind Rotation

  • East (Player 1) is the dealer. East is the prevailing wind in Round 1.
  • Dealer wins → winds stay. Same dealer plays again.
  • Any other player wins → all seat winds rotate anti-clockwise one step.
  • After all 4 players have held East, prevailing wind changes: East → South → West → North.
  • Flower 1 & Season 1 = East seat. Flower 2 & Season 2 = South. Flower 3 & Season 3 = West. Flower 4 & Season 4 = North.

High-Risk Discard Scenarios (Pao 包 — Shooter Pays All)

In these situations the discarder pays the FULL payout (including what the other two losers would normally pay):

  • Dragon Pao 包三元: Player A has 2 dragon Pong/Kangs exposed. Player B discards the 3rd dragon type → Player B pays all if A wins.
  • Wind Pao 包四喜: Player A has 3 wind Pong/Kangs exposed. Player B discards the 4th wind type → Player B pays all if A wins.
  • Limit Pao 包满贯: Player A already has (Limit−1) points exposed. Player B discards a tile that brings A to the limit → Player B pays all if A wins.
  • Full Flush Pao 包清一色: Player A has 3–4 sets of same suit exposed. Player B discards a same-suit tile → Player B pays all if A wins.
  • Kang Pao 包杠: With ≤7 tiles in wall, Player B discards a fresh tile (never discarded before), Player A Kangs it → Player B pays all Kang instant payment.
  • Fresh Tile Pao 包生: With ≤5 tiles in wall, Player B discards a fresh tile and Player A wins with it → Player B pays all winnings.

Penalties

  • Wrong Win Claim 诈胡: Player claims a win incorrectly. Must pay 5pts (or limit) to all 3 players — discarder gets double.
  • Short Hand 小相公: Fewer than 13 tiles in hand. Player forfeits right to win for current round (but can still Kang).
  • Long Hand 大相公: More than 13 tiles in hand (or drew wrong tile). Player forfeits right to win AND right to Kang for current round.
  • Sacred Discard 回头牌: If you discard a tile, you cannot claim the same tile from others' discards for win/Pong in the same go-around.
  • Missed Discard 过水牌: If you miss claiming a tile, you cannot claim the same tile from other players' discards for win/Pong in the same go-around.
